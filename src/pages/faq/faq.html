Help
  1. Q. I can't get the lessons.
    A. Please head over to the lesson page, ensure that you are connected to the internet. Then wait the application to download the lessons. On a good network coverage it should not take more than a minute. You may need to revisit the lesson page if no lessons yet.
  2. Q. How do I access the lessons offline?
    A. Once you get the lessons, you don't need any network as the lessons are stored in the local storage of your device. You only need internet when you got to get lessons update.
  3. Q. How do I get Swahili lesson version?
    A. Go to settings, change the language to Swahili. Head over to the lessons page, it will have fetched the Swahili version.
  4. Q. How do I share a lesson link to facebook, whatsapp or twitter?
    A. Select the lesson in which you want to share the link, there are buttons on the bottom right of the lesson page. Click on the button, then select to share with facebook, whatsapp or twitter.
  5. Q. What is in the calendar page?
    A. Calendar page consists of Gregorian date converter to the Biblical dates. The arrows << goes back one week and the arrows >> goes forward one week. The arrow < goes back one day and > goes forward by a day.

    There is a datepicker in the center of the arrows to enable you to pick any date of choice

    To view holidays recorded in Leviticus 23 tap on the link (Holidays)

    You can navigate one year forward or an year back with the arrows on the top

    For description about any given holiday, tap on the name of the holiday.


  6. Q. How do I report a bug, suggests, or send any feedback?
    A. You can do so by heading over to the settings page, tap on the Feedback and you will be redirected to default e-mail application. Sends an e-mail, we will appreciate.
